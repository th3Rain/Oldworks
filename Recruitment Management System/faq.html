Frequently Asked Questions

Why must my photo be 450px by 450px?
This ensures the clarity of the image so the job application board can identify who is being awarded.
What is Scholastica?
R.M.S solutions is your One Stop job application where one profile can be used to apply for multiple jobs. This saves you the stress of having to upload your documents every time and keeps you updated on ongoing job application.
What happens if i do not qualify or meet the criteria of a job application?
Ensure your profile is filled correctly and truthfully, you can also apply for other jobs that match your status
What is "NIN"?
NIN is the National Identification Number being coordinated by the National Identity Management Commission
I am interested in a job application on R.M.S solutions .How do I apply?
Register with a valid e-mail address and create a profile on R.M.S solutions , then apply for the job.
I made a mistake. How do I edit my profile details?
If you have not applied for any job, you can still edit your profile details. To edit your profile details after applying for a job, send an email to admin@rmssolutions.com from the email account you used to create your job profile stating your case. Ensure the email is titled appropriately. For example “Correction of details on job profile” 
My academic qualification is not on the list. What should I do?
Send an email to admin@rmssolutions.com stating the overall standard of the qualification, Institutions offering the certification and the certification details.
How do I know if I am eligible for a job?
The eligibility criteria of each each job description is clearly defined in the Requirements page of the scheme, you can also contact admin@rmssolutions.com for more details.
I am unable to upload my documents. What can I do?
Confirm your internet connectivity, ensure you are not using a mobile device that is not enabled for such activity, try to use a different browser software, log out of your account and log back in. If none of these solutions work, please contact our customer care lines for guidance. Also confirm the documents are in the right format and are not above 200Kb.
How do I download my profile form?
After application for a job, return to the overview page of your profile to download your profile form. Note: The same profile form is used for all job applications on R.M.S solutions
How do I upload my profile form?
After your referee(s) has signed the downloaded profile form. Scan the document, login to your job profile and upload the signed document
What is the meaning of bank sort code and is it necessary?
A bank sort code is a nine digits number that identifies a specific branch of a bank. The first three digits are the bank’s standard code, the next digit is the location of the bank and the last five digits are for the bank branch. It facilitates online transfers.
How can I know if I have successfully applied for a job?
You will get an email confirmation in your email account, you will also see it in your R.M.S solution account overview page and a copy is saved to your messages in your R.M.S solution Account.
How can I obtain my NIN and is it compulsory?
Yes your NIN is very compulsory as it helps the job recruitment board confirm your identity
I have not been selected even though others have been selected. Why?
Most times the cause of selection delays is associated with inability to confirm a candidates current academic status as at when due, hence selection cannot be done as at when due. To rectify this, the affected applicants should call R.M.S solutions customer care to confirm his status. Please note R.M.S solutions does not recruit applicants. They help as 3rd party for organizations wanting to recruit.

  •    In the case where a candidates status is invalid meaning his or her status for requirment is below the award criteria of the scholarship and candidate has evidence that this is not correct. Do I need more than one account on R.M.S solutions?
NO, with one account you can apply for all jobs within your qualification. If you create more than one account, you will be automatically ineligible to receive any job award
